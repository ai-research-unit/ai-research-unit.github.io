# Human organization — Specialisation Module

Reads with the Human Framework, never alone. Adds specialised aspects to each domain.

## D01. Physical Domain

__Footprint & Sites:__ Where it operates — locations, premises, the geography of the operation, including a distributed or remote arrangement — and what each site's setting and hazards impose on the work.

__Workplace Conditions:__ The physical environment of the work itself — light, noise, safety, density, the shop floor or its deliberate absence — as the daily stage members work on.

__Premises Wear & Site Burden:__ The residue — deferred upkeep of the premises and the degraded ground the operation leaves on its sites; cleared, carried, or accreting; possibly an informative null.

## D02. Biological Domain

__Headcount & Composition:__ The workforce as bodies — size, age structure, tenure mix — the human stock the operation runs on, at the grain the organization is read.

__Vitality & Workload:__ Energy against demands — hours, intensity, the sustainable pace or its breach — the biological load the work places on the bodies that do it.

__Renewal Metabolism:__ Hiring against attrition — the membrane's flow: who joins, who leaves, at what rate, and whether intake replaces in kind what departure removes.

__Burnout & Spent Capacity:__ The residue — exhaustion, injury, and the wear that churn leaves behind; absorbed by renewal, treated, or accreting past what the workforce can carry.

## D03. Economic Domain

__Funding Engine & Model:__ How money comes in — sales, dues, grants, budget allocation — the streams, their concentration, and their reliability, whatever the organization's form.

__Cost Structure & Margin:__ What running it costs against what comes in — the burn, the margin or deficit, and where the money actually goes as against where the plan says it goes.

__Plant, Tools & Product:__ The productive apparatus — machinery, systems, codebase, product line — what the organization makes and makes with.

__Reserves & Runway:__ The cushion — how long the operation holds if its main stream stops, the buffer or its absence said plainly.

__Exchange Position:__ Suppliers and customers as flows — concentration on each side, the terms held, the dependence carried.

__Waste, Sunk Costs & Plant Debt:__ The residue — the discards and emissions the operation routes outward, the sunk investments still billing, and the accumulated shortcuts in plant and product: technical debt as deferred quality.

## D04. Social Domain

__Cliques, Camps & Old Guards:__ The informal groupings — founder loyalists, site rivalries, professional tribes — the lines between them and the traffic across them.

__Unwritten Norms:__ How things are actually done — the informal code of hours, candor, conflict, and favor — beneath the written policy.

__Ownership, Governance & Real Power:__ Who ultimately owns or controls — shareholders, members, founders, a parent body, the state — and what they demand of it; and the formal authority as drawn against where decisions actually get made — the shadow structure named plainly as the governance face of the charter-floor gap.

__Decision Process & Succession:__ How choices move — committees, sign-offs, escalation paths — governance throughput against the decisions arriving, and where they queue; and how leadership transfers and how much of the organization is parked in one office — the founder question at institutional scale.

__External Relations & Oversight:__ Regulators, partners, parent bodies, and the host community — the standing relations and oversights the organization operates under, read from its side of each interface.

__Grievance, Sidelined & Spent Authority:__ The residue — unaired resentments, the passed-over and the pushed-out, the friction stock the fabric carries, and the lapsed mandates, reorganization wreckage, and the units or conduct leadership cannot in practice govern; serviced, vented, or accreting.

## D05. Emotional Domain

__Will, Drive & Resolve:__ The organization's collective will — the ambition and appetite that actually move it, the resolve to act, bet, or grow against the pull to coast or retreat, and the leadership drive that concentrates and directs it; read off what it pursues, not what it proclaims.

__Trust & Felt Cohesion:__ The working trust among members — across teams, between floor and leadership — the radius cooperation actually runs on, and which way it is moving.

__Morale & Temper:__ The ambient temper of the place — pride, siege, drift, swagger — the feeling of working there as it actually shows, not as the brochure renders it.

__Conviction & Vocation:__ How much the work is believed in — the share who serve a cause against the share who work a job — and what sacrifice the felt commitment can genuinely summon when asked.

__Disaffection & Spent Will:__ The residue — the cynicism, demoralisation, and curdled trust the operation leaves: the disengaged, the checked-out, the morale spent past recovery; serviced, vented, or accreting, and feeding the Social grievance stock.

## D06. Informational Domain

__Channels & Meeting Fabric:__ How word moves inside — meetings, messages, documents, the all-hands and the corridor — the reach and speed of the internal channel across sites and ranks.

__Records & Systems of Memory:__ What is written down and findable — documentation, archives, the systems of record — against what lives only in heads.

__Candor & Upward Signal:__ What may be said and what travels up — whether bad news reaches deciders or dies en route, and the honesty of the figures the organization keeps about itself.

__External Voice & Circulating Reputation:__ The outward channels and what circulates about the organization — press, reviews, the record in the world.

__Noise, Rumor & Stale Record:__ The residue — the rumor mill, contradictory documents, dead archives and outdated records accreting against findability; cleared or compounding.

## D07. Moral Domain

__External Law & Compliance Load:__ The regulation, licensing, and law the organization operates under, and the compliance burden of staying within it — the binding outer code and its cost in conduct.

__Internal Code & Contracts:__ The written rules of membership — policies, handbooks, terms of employment or belonging — the codified order beneath the unwritten norms.

__Enforcement & Internal Justice:__ How the code is actually applied — discipline, grievance handling, the evenness of enforcement up and down the hierarchy — practice read against policy.

__Impunity & The Protected:__ The residue — rules unevenly enforced, the untouchable performer, the case left unresolved; the gap that feeds the Social grievance stock.

## D08. Intellectual Domain

__Core Competence & Craft:__ What the organization is actually good at — the capabilities that produce its work, at demonstrated depth, as against the competence it claims.

__Skill Distribution & Bench:__ How widely competence is spread — the bench behind each critical skill, what lives in few heads.

__Learning & Method Renewal:__ Training, development, and the uptake of new method — whether the craft frontier is being worked or coasted on.

__Obsolete Competence & Walked-Out Knowledge:__ The residue — skills the work no longer needs, and the knowledge that left with departures undocumented.

## D09. Cultural Domain

__Founding Story & Identity:__ The origin legend and who the organization takes itself to be — the celebrated self and its heroes.

__Style, Symbols & House Voice:__ The expressive surface — the aesthetic of its spaces and product, the emblems and lore, the house jargon as identity marker.

__Hollow Values & Stale Story:__ The residue — the poster values no one believes, the origin story outgrown, the brand gone stale; renewed, retired, or left as inert decoration; possibly an informative null.

## D10. Spiritual Domain

__Mission as Held:__ What the organization is for, as actually believed — the purpose members would name unprompted — read against the chartered mission, with the gap between them the deepest charter-floor reading on the map.

__Mission Gone Cold:__ The residue — the purpose hollowed, the believers departed or turned mercenary, and what fills the space belief left; possibly an informative null in a frankly transactional shop.
