# __Model Framework__
__Summary:__ The object the specialised frameworks project — one bounded human subject in a state, partitioned into ten orthogonal domains each closing on a residue — and the projection operator that derives a specialised framework from it.

# __Explanations__
## __M01. What This Is__
This is not a profile; it is the object the specialised frameworks project, and the operation that produces a projection. The object is one bounded human subject in a state — a society, a place, a culture, an institution, a movement, real or fictional — fully described by ten orthogonal domains, each closing on a residue. The specialised frameworks are projections of this one object; nothing in them lies outside it. The body above states the object as a partition of ten descriptions; the modules state the invariants, the routing, the rendering, and the projection operator. This document is the model the specialised frameworks are built from; where any framework's restatement of the invariants or routing diverges from it, this document governs. Each framework follows the model but never refers to it, and is written to be read and used on its own, with no need for this document. The frameworks restate the invariants in their own words, but never name the object or the projection operator — that meta-structure is set out here alone. The four modes are a property of any projection's coordinate, not of the lens:<br><br>
### Static Snapshot
The subject's standing state at a fixed coordinate.<br><br>
### Discrete Event
A rapid, disruptive turning point, bracketed from rupture to settling.<br><br>
### Long-Term Trend
A multi-generational shift with no single flashpoint, bracketed across the transition.<br><br>
### Speculative Construction
A coherent extrapolation or invention from established limits.<br><br>
A projection may also carry one lens-specific operating regime beyond these four — a mode of use the lens demands though the coordinate does not, such as a discovery regime that suspends the partition to surface or invent candidate entities; it is named in that projection alone and is not a fifth coordinate-mode.<br><br>

## __M02. The Six Invariants__
### Orthogonality
The ten domains partition the subject — exhaustive (everything true of it maps somewhere) and exclusive (everything maps to exactly one); the seams are routed by the rules in M03, on the recurring cut between a medium and the significance placed on it.<br><br>
### Aliveness
Read each line as a standing level, a direction of change, and a capacity-to-load ratio. The direction-of-change reading is the one slot a projection sharpens — into operational trend, drift-and-volatility, seasonal cycle, or developmental phase.<br><br>
### Metabolic closure
Every domain's description closes by naming the residue it discharges — and the Physical domain closes on a dual residue, the sink it provides for the rest of the system's discharge and the weathering it accumulates in its own right — read for whether it is cleared or left to accrete; a thin projection may close a domain on an informative null, but never omit the residue.<br><br>
### Significance ordering
Foundational to surface within each domain and across the ten, residue last; the single load-bearing factor is named in the Summary, never signalled by reordering.<br><br>
### Scale and tempo invariance
The same ten domains fit a band or an empire, a place or a culture, an afternoon or ten millennia; the set is never extended or pruned, only answered differently.<br><br>
### Evidence discipline
Real subjects are bounded by best evidence, speculative ones extrapolated from material limits; inference is kept distinct from attestation, an absent function is recorded as a null, nothing is fabricated.<br><br>

## __M03. Cross-Domain Routing__
The partition resolves nearly every ambiguous fact by one recurring cut: the medium is separated from the significance placed on it. The material, the apparatus, the channel, the body each sit in the domain that owns the thing; the permission, status, rule, meaning, method, or cosmology attached to it sits in the domain that owns the significance. Applied to the seams:<br><br>
- **Physical / Economic** — the endowment of substances, energy, and terrain is Physical; its conversion into work, goods, provisioning, and cost is Economic.
- **Biological / Economic** — bodies, health, and population are Biological; the food, care, and goods supply that sustains them is Economic.
- **Biological / Cultural-Social-Judicial** — the body is Biological; the meaning placed on it, beauty, status, or taboo, is Cultural, Social, or Judicial.
- **Economic / Social** — the holding and flow of goods is Economic; the status meaning of holdings is Social.
- **Economic / Judicial** — the material flow is Economic; who is permitted to do what with it is Judicial.
- **Economic / Political** — production, exchange, and the surplus flow they generate, including tax, tribute, and rent collected and reallocated, are Economic; the sovereign authority's power to extract it is Political.
- **Social / Judicial** — informal norm and custom are Social; the codified rule and its adjudication are Judicial.
- **Social / Political** — informal groupings are Social; the sovereign structure above them and its force is Political.
- **Political / Judicial** — the apparatus of authority and force is Political; the rules it enforces are Judicial.
- **Political-Judicial / Informational** — the binding command and its justification are Political or Judicial; the framing and carrying of the message is Informational.
- **Informational / Intellectual** — the carrying of meaning is Informational; the truth-seeking and method behind it are Intellectual.
- **Informational / Cultural** — the channel and medium are Informational; the expressive form is Cultural.
- **Intellectual / Economic** — knowing and method are Intellectual; their embodiment in tools and production is Economic.
- **Intellectual / Spiritual** — the empirical paradigm is Intellectual; the metaphysical frame is Spiritual.
- **Cultural / Spiritual** — the expression and self-image are Cultural; the cosmology behind them is Spiritual.
- **Spiritual / Political** — belief and the holy are Spiritual; the clerical apparatus as power is Political.
- **Spiritual / Social** — devotion to the sacred is Spiritual; the rite as social custom is Social.<br><br>
Where a fact still seems to fit two cells, the medium-against-significance cut decides it; sharpen the seam rather than duplicate the fact.<br><br>

## __M04. The Projection Operator__
A specialisation is a projection of the object, fully specified by five choices:<br><br>
### 1. Unit
Which bounded subject is read (an operational state, a destination, a cultural identity, an organisation, a market, a life).<br><br>
### 2. Lens
The single question put to every domain, which decides what each line reports (how it operates, how it bears on an outsider living here, how it looks, how it performs). The lens is also a weighting: it expands the domains at its centre of gravity into many lines and collapses the rest toward a single line or an informative null. This weighting, not any new domain, is what makes two projections look unalike.<br><br>
### 3. Coordinate
The handle that bounds the subject (temporal window, place-and-date, entity-and-phase).<br><br>
### 4. Summary focus
What the load-bearing factor is called under this lens (binding constraint, central tension, organising through-line).<br><br>
### 5. Apparatus
Reading-specific machinery, added only when the lens demands it (confidence tags for a stake-bearing outside-in reading; generative concreteness and a phase coordinate for an aesthetic reading; none for the neutral operational reading).<br><br>
Then populate each domain through the lens, apply the weighting, keep every residue or an explicit null, and render in the shared format. The ten domains, six invariants, and routing never change.<br><br>

## __M05. Rendering__
A projection renders as plain text, continuous and dense with zero blank lines. Line 1 is the header — one bold span holding the Coordinate (whatever handle bounds the subject: a temporal window, a place and date, an entity and phase) and the Subject Title, separated by a spaced em-dash; parsers strip the leading "# " and the outer __, then split at the first em-dash, coordinate left and title right, so the title contains no em-dash; Line 2 is the Summary, no trailing break; section lines begin ## PXX. Title; each domain holds one or more lines, each a bolded key, a colon, one space, the payload, and a single trailing <br><br>; the domain's residue is its last line. No line-number prefixes, brackets, tags, or markers — lines are tracked by position. Payloads run roughly 20-40 words. Completeness is by domain: all ten present, in order, each closed on its residue. This base, being the schema rather than an instance, states each domain as a single unkeyed description closing on its residue.<br><br>

## __M06. Quick Reference & Checklist__
The object: one bounded human subject in a state, partitioned into ten orthogonal domains, each closing on a residue. Any specialised framework is a projection of it; this document is the object plus the projection operator — the model the specialised frameworks follow without ever referring to it.<br><br>
Six invariants: orthogonality (a partition), aliveness, metabolic closure, significance ordering, scale-and-tempo invariance, evidence discipline.<br><br>
Routing: the seams resolve on one recurring cut, the medium against the significance placed on it; M03 is the full table.<br><br>
A projection fixes five things: Unit, Lens (with its weighting), Coordinate, Summary focus, Apparatus — then populates, prunes by the weighting, keeps every residue, and renders in the shared format.<br><br>
Before deriving a projection: the five choices fixed; the ten domains held as an exhaustive, exclusive partition; the lens's weighting applied so the right domains expand and the rest collapse; every residue kept or made an explicit null; rendering clean.<br><br>


# __Coordinate — Subject Title__
__Summary:__ The subject in one breath — what it is and the handle bounding it, the single load-bearing factor doing the most causal work (binding constraint, central tension, or organising through-line, by the reading), the margin it holds against shocks, and the principal direction of change.
## __P01. Physical Environment__
The inert material stage — terrain and location, climate and atmosphere, hazard exposure, and the standing endowment of substances and energy the subject can draw on. It discharges a physical residue: the sink of what the environment absorbs and the wear it leaves — waste load, pollution, weathering, ruin.<br><br>
## __P02. Biological Environment__
The living substrate — the bodies and their type, the disease and mortality baseline, vitality and the capacity to heal, and reproduction and density against the carrying ceiling. It discharges the residue of decline and dependency: the standing load of age, infirmity, and death the life-cycle leaves, and how it is met.<br><br>
## __P03. Economic Environment__
The conversion of endowment into life and surplus — the provisioning floor, the energy and means of production, exchange and logistics, the value-medium that stores and defers worth, and the concentration and spread of wealth. It discharges waste and excess: refuse, effluent, and the detritus of consumption and surplus.<br><br>
## __P04. Social Environment__
The informal fabric between persons — the radius of trust and cohesion, the stratification of class and status, the norms, customs, and bearing that govern conduct, and the families, guilds, and factions that form below the whole. It discharges friction and the outcast: the grievance the fabric throws off and the excluded it leaves.<br><br>
## __P05. Political Environment__
Sovereign coordination and force — where power sits and how reliably it transfers, the administrative capacity and integrity that give it reach, external relations beyond the boundary, and the organised means of violence. It discharges disorder and fallen power: the violence the monopoly fails to absorb, and what spent authority leaves behind.<br><br>
## __P06. Informational Environment__
The movement of meaning — the shared code that makes minds mutually legible, the channels and their reach, the media that fix and store messages across time, and the framing and control of what may be said. It discharges noise and the watched: the distortion, corruption, and surveillance the channel throws off.<br><br>
## __P07. Judicial Environment__
The binding ought — the moral foundations and values beneath any code, the codified law and the taboos that mark the forbidden, and the adjudication, rights, and enforcement that apply them. It discharges impunity and transgression: the gap between justice owed and delivered, and the marks left on the transgressor.<br><br>
## __P08. Intellectual Environment__
The understanding of reality — the inquiry and paradigm that frame what counts as knowing, the applied method and craft that turn it into technique, and the stock, schooling, and distribution of skill. It discharges obsolescence and loss: the superseded theory shed and the knowledge decayed or broken in transmission.<br><br>
## __P09. Cultural Environment__
The expressive surface — the mythos, memory, and identity that fix who the subject takes itself to be, the artistic and material expression through which it externalises that, and the collective mood and disposition that colour it. It discharges form-exhaustion: the hollowing of once-potent symbols and styles into cliché, anomie, and staleness.<br><br>
## __P10. Spiritual Environment__
The metaphysical frame — the cosmology and existential outlook that place the subject in reality, the ritual and sacred through which that is enacted, and the religious institutions and doctrinal authority that hold it. It discharges disenchantment: the residue where a cosmology loses its grip — secularising drift, the void, the sacred gone cold.<br><br>
