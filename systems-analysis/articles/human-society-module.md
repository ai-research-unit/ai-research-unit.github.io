# Human Society — Specialisation Module

Reads with the Human Framework, never alone. Adds specialised aspects to each domain.

## D01. Physical Domain

__Geography & Spatial Friction:__ The fixed topography, transit corridors and chokepoints, hydrological networks, and soil mechanics — the slow-moving spatial arena and the baseline friction it imposes on movement, settlement, and defense before any human modification.

__Climate Regime & Volatility:__ The prevailing temperature, rainfall, and seasonal regime, its interannual variability, and the direction and pace of any secular drift — warming, cooling, aridification — that widens or narrows the band the system is provisioned for.

__Hazard Regime & Exposure:__ Exposure to acute environmental shocks — seismic, volcanic, flood, drought, storm, wildfire — the frequency and intensity of the regime, and how much margin settlement, infrastructure, and provisioning hold against it.

__Resource & Energy Endowment:__ The availability, accessibility, and concentration of the raw materials and energy sources the system can draw on, and the rate of drawdown against the stock that remains — the endowment as a standing stock.

__Waste Absorption & Sink Capacity:__ The saturation of the air, water, and soil that must absorb the system's heat, effluent, and discards, the degradation already imprinted on the landscape, and how much absorptive capacity remains against the loads arriving — the sink side; and the feedback a saturated sink returns, degradation past a threshold looping back into the system as scarcity, rising cost, or a trigger of unrest rather than sitting inert.

## D02. Biological Domain

__Pathogen Load & Mortality Baseline:__ Endemic and epidemic disease vectors, sanitary exposure, and the background death rate they impose, including the direction in which that rate is trending and how close it sits to outrunning renewal.

__Nutrition & Physiological Vitality:__ The actual nutritional status, stature, and physical robustness of bodies — the biological outcome of provisioning, and whether it is improving or degrading.

__Care, Healing & Recovery Capacity:__ The standing capacity to prevent, treat, and recover from injury, illness, and bodily breakdown — from wound-tending, midwifery, and folk remedy to institutional medicine — and how evenly that capacity reaches the population against the load arriving.

__Reproductive Biology & Pairing Dynamics:__ Physiological fertility, mating and pair-bonding patterns, and the selective reproductive pressures shaping the population — the biological capacity to reproduce, prior to the demographic accounting of whether it does.

__Demographic Renewal & Density:__ Birth-to-death replacement ratios, age structure, the pace of population turnover, migration flows together with the pressures driving them and the absorption gain or drain they impose, and spatial crowding measured against the carrying ceiling.

__Attrition & Dependency Overhang:__ The standing stock of dependents the demographic cycle leaves in its wake — orphaned, widowed, aged, chronically infirm — and whether the productive cohort can absorb that load or is dragged below replacement by it.

## D03. Economic Domain

__Subsistence & Provisioning Floor:__ Baseline material survival — food, water, shelter, fuel — how close the population sits to the carrying-capacity edge, and the household pooling strategies that buffer it.

__Energy Capture & Conversion:__ The portfolio of power sources actually harnessed — human and animal muscle, fire, water, wind, fossil, electrical — the efficiency with which the endowment is transduced into useful work, and the resulting per-capita work ceiling that gates everything built above it.

__Labor Organization & Coercion:__ How human labor is structured, specialized, and mobilized — slave, serf, corvée, wage, or contract — the degree of coercion versus compensation in the labor relation, and how that mix drifts as conditions tighten or loosen.

__Production & Surplus Capacity:__ The productive capacity and output ceiling of agrarian, workshop, or industrial activity, the flow of surplus generated above bare subsistence per cycle, and the phase that flow sits in — expansion or contraction, over-extended or slack.

__Storage, Reserves & Buffering:__ The accumulated buffer held against shock — granaries, inventories, treasuries, strategic reserves — and whether stockpile depth and the instruments for pooling risk suffice to absorb a failed harvest, supply shock, or demand collapse; and, set against that positive stock, the negative buffer — the standing debt and pre-committed claims the treasury already carries and how much borrowing capacity remains before the next shock — the polity's own balance sheet as a limit on its capacity to act.

__Exchange, Trade & Logistics:__ Market structures, supply-chain transit, distribution networks, clearing velocity, and the infrastructure bottlenecks that gate them; and the external dependence these create — reliance on inflows or outlets another actor could sever by blockade, embargo, or closure of a route, and the vulnerability that dependence opens.

__Value Storage, Credit & Deferred Exchange:__ The means by which value is stored, transferred, and lent across time — unit of account and its purchasing power where one exists, debasement trends, credit and debt, capital formation, and, below any monetary instrument, the deferred-reciprocity obligations ("share now, owe later") that carry the same function.

__Surplus Extraction & Redistribution:__ How surplus is appropriated and reallocated — tax, tribute, rent, seigniorage at the state scale; communal allocation of a large kill or harvest at the band scale — the load this places on the productive base, and the remaining room to raise more before that base resists or breaks: the revenue-raising side of fiscal room to act.

__Wealth Distribution & Asset Defense:__ The concentration of property, land, capital, or positional advantage across tiers, whether it is widening or leveling, and the strategies used to shield holdings from theft, inflation, or sovereign confiscation.

__Byproducts & Waste Routing:__ The volume and toxicity of the waste, effluent, and discards production throws off, and the routing decision — dispersed, recycled, sequestered, or dumped — that sends them toward the physical sink or back onto the resource base. On the financial side, the accumulated mispricing surplus leaves behind — malinvestment, bad debt, price-drift — cleared or left to accrete.

## D04. Social Domain

__Kinship, Family & Inheritance:__ The constructs governing marriage, household formation, and kin alliance, and the rules of inheritance and property transfer across generations — the slow-changing scaffold of who is bound to whom.

__Association & Mutual Aid:__ The voluntary fabric below kin and state — guilds, communes, neighbourhoods, and associations that enforce norms and coordinate action, the informal safety nets, reciprocity, charity, and communal pooling that sustain the populace, and the standing structures through which reliance and cooperation extend beyond kin — and whether that fabric is thickening or thinning.

__Leisure, Play & Sociability:__ The organization of non-work time, shared festive spaces, and the recurring rituals of play through which bonds and identity are forged in practice.

__Stratification, Status & Group Structure:__ Division by class, caste, ethnicity, and lineage; the status hierarchy; the rigidity or permeability of its boundaries and whether they are hardening or dissolving; and the structural factional or tribal blocs active in the window.

__Administrative Capacity & Institutional Integrity:__ The standing civil apparatus through which the polity is administered and represented, at home and abroad — the offices and bureaucracy; the representative and administrative institutions (assemblies, electoral systems, parties as organizations) and how authority is formally arranged and distributed among them; the procedure by which office is filled and passed; and the diplomatic service and standing relations with other bodies — together with its competence and reach, the thoroughness of collection and record-keeping, corruption levels, and whether decision-access is monopolised or dispersed.

__Coercive Apparatus & Means of Force:__ The organized capacity for violence — military, police, fortification — the premier weaponry and its fabrication and distribution constraints, how the apparatus is structured, supplied, and held, and how that capacity measures against the forces it may face — the relative balance, not the absolute stock alone.

__Discharge, Disorder & Decay:__ The channels — mediation, mobility, charity, ritual release — that absorb and dissipate social friction before it hardens, and the friction and dysfunction left when they fail: the grievance unmetabolized and the excluded the fabric casts out; the breakdown of public order the apparatus cannot contain — crime, theft, banditry, riot, vendetta, factional violence, predation, and rebellion; and the sclerosis or wreckage of spent and ossified apparatus — cleared or left to accrete.

## D05. Emotional Domain

__Public Sentiment & Behavioral Temper:__ Prevailing public emotions, shared anxieties or confidence, the radius of interpersonal trust and the willingness to rely on others beyond kin, the baseline risk perception and behavioral temper, and whether the mood and that reach of trust are darkening or lifting, widening or contracting.

__Leadership & Personal Authority:__ The leaders and the personal authority, pull, or charisma through which they concentrate and direct will — the figures who command allegiance.

__Legitimacy as Lived Allegiance:__ The actually-held belief in the right of rulers and the order to command — allegiance versus disaffection, how deeply felt, and whether it is eroding; the standing reservoir of consent the active will draws on.

__Political Will & Collective Resolve:__ The system's capacity for unified willed action and the contest to direct it — the struggle for power and over succession, whether the ruling group itself coheres or fractures and how open or contested the handover of power is, who holds ultimate authority and how firmly, the drive to govern and act, and the readiness to mobilise, rally, strike, or fight.

__External Relations & Posture:__ The system's directed will toward other powers — alignment and antagonism, the appetite for expansion, deterrence, or retreat, the felt rivalry and ambition that set its stance abroad, and the leverage each side holds over the other — the economic or coercive hold that dependency confers.

__Disaffection, Apathy & Spent Will:__ The standing distrust, estrangement, apathy, and exhaustion the affective drive leaves when it is spent or thwarted — the collapse of collective will, and the disaffection that, uncontained, feeds the disorder in Social — re-anchored or left to settle.

## D06. Informational Domain

__Communication Accessibility:__ The existence and breadth of a shared communicative code — imitative gesture, vocalization, speech, sign, symbol, or script — and the mutual intelligibility it affords: who can encode and decode it, who is excluded by incapacity, foreignness, or disability, and whether the shared ground is widening or fragmenting.

__Channels & Transmission Velocity:__ The physical, acoustic, or institutional means governing the speed and reach of data transmission across space, and how broadly that reach extends across strata.

__Record, Storage & Persistence:__ The media and institutions that fix information across time — archives, registers, libraries, ledgers, durable copies, or living memory alone — their retrievability and durability, and whether the record survives shock and turnover or decays and is lost.

__Framing, Censorship & Discourse Norms:__ Message framing, operational censorship, communication etiquette, and the conversational taboos that shape what can be said and how — and whether that permitted band is widening or narrowing.

__Reception, Attention & Amplification:__ Audience consumption patterns, the dynamics of amplification and virality, and the attention-span constraints that gate what propagates.

__Signal Integrity & Noise Load:__ The mechanisms for clearing obsolete, corrupted, or contradictory data; the prevailing signal-to-noise condition; the reach of surveillance and the watched; and whether verification capacity keeps pace with information volume.

## D07. Moral Domain

__Moral Foundations & Sense of Justice:__ The society's baseline notions of right and wrong, fair and legitimate — the shared conscience that underwrites or undermines the formal code.

__Law, Codification & Regulatory Evolution:__ The current state and active evolution of formal laws, edicts, and binding unwritten taboos governing conduct.

__Adjudication, Liability & Sanction:__ How disputes are judged and culpability assigned, and the sanction the rule warrants — and whether judgment keeps pace with the caseload.

__Rights, Protections & Compliance Burden:__ The scope of individual autonomy and protected rights, the predictability of the rules, and the compliance drag the system imposes on ordinary conduct.

__Impunity & Unredressed Wrong:__ The standing gap between justice formally owed and actually delivered — impunity for the powerful, unenforced judgments, backlog, wrongful outcomes — and whether redress capacity clears the deficit or lets it accumulate.

## D08. Intellectual Domain

__Inquiry, Theory & Paradigm:__ Formal investigation of the natural and human world, the reigning explanatory paradigms, and the location of the active discovery frontier.

__Applied Knowledge & Technical Method:__ How abstract discoveries are engineered into reproducible methods, tool-designs, and work-blueprints — the technique actually in use during the window.

__Education, Knowledge Stock & Skill Distribution:__ Schooling frameworks, literacy levels, the accumulated and preserved stock of knowledge, and how widely technical skill is distributed across strata — and whether that stock and spread are growing or contracting.

__Obsolescence & Knowledge Decay:__ The shedding of superseded theory and dead technique, the rate at which accumulated knowledge is lost through decayed records, broken transmission, or skill attrition, and whether error and untested dogma are cleared or left to accrete.

## D09. Cultural Domain

__Mythos, Memory & Foundational Identity:__ Deep worldviews, ancestral symbols, origin narratives, and the historic traumas and triumphs that shape the collective sense of who a people are — the slow bedrock beneath the era's surface mood.

__Legitimating Narrative & Ideology:__ The arguments, historical narratives, and ideologies deployed to justify the prevailing order and sustain the status quo — the story of the order's right to exist.

__Artistic Expression & Material Culture:__ The visual and material surface of the era read whole, the made-object world of artifact, dress, and building, the color palette and its accents, the texture and finish through which materials are made to read, the symbolism encoded in emblem, color, and figure, the sartorial tradition of drape, tailoring, jewelry, and worn arms, and the architectural idiom and spatial philosophy dividing public from private space.

__Form Exhaustion & Meaning Decay:__ The hollowing of once-potent symbols, rituals, and styles into cliché or dead convention, the build-up of anomie where shared meaning has eroded, and whether forms are renewed, repurposed, or left as inert residue.

## D10. Spiritual Domain

__Cosmology & Existential Outlook:__ The metaphysical model of reality and the human place within it, and the shifting balance among fatalism, piety, doubt, and secularism.

__Ritual, Devotion & Sacred Practice:__ Ceremonial actions, devotional habits, and the daily and communal infrastructure through which the sacred is enacted.

__Religious Institutions & Doctrinal Authority:__ Clerical hierarchies, institutional property and reach, and whether doctrinal orthodoxy is being consolidated and enforced or contested and fractured.

__Spiritual Diffusion, Syncretism & Displacement:__ The spread, blending, fracturing, replacement, or abandonment of spiritual belief systems across population and geography, and the direction in which the dominant cosmology's reach is moving.

__Disenchantment & Sacred Discharge:__ The residue left where a cosmology loses its grip — secularizing drift, eroded transcendence, the existential void where the sacred no longer answers — and whether the spiritual need is re-anchored, redirected, or left to settle as standing disenchantment.
