Systems Analysis

The idea is simple: anything true of a human system fits into one of ten domains — its physical setting, its bodies and health, its economy, its social fabric, its politics, its information flows, its rules, its knowledge, its culture, and its beliefs.

How to use

  1. Basic Use:

Example 1: Assess Chiang Mai as a destination for a digital nomad

You just copy the full page Human Framework in the prompt of an AI, and you add : "Apply the framework to Chiang Mai Mai as a destination for a digital nomad in 2026".

You will get a response like this.
